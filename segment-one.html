Segment One

Class Information

Segment One is the class that can be taken when a student reaches 14 years and 8 months of age by/on the first day of class. Students will need a copy of their birth certificate, the Segment One registration form completed, and the Segment One contract completed for the first day of class. The cost is $330. Cash and checks accepted. Checks can be made out to Lansing Area Driving School. Material will be provided to the students.
