Segment Two

Class Information

Segment two is the class students can take when they have had a Level 1 license for at least 90 days before the first day of class. Students will need to provide the Segment 2 contract completed and their Level 1 license for the first day of class. Students should have completed 30 hours of drive time with at least 2 hours of driving at night before the class starts. The cost is $60. Cash and checks accepted. Checks can be made out to Lansing Area Driving School. Material will be provided to students.
